# BlurBubble: Open Spatial Privacy & Compliance Protocol (Beta Sandbox)

Welcome to **BlurBubble** (Stop Recording Me) — a modern, open-source tactical privacy shield and decentralized compliance manager designed to defend citizens, localized spaces, and physical/digital assets from unauthorized biometric, audio, and video recording.

This repository is a **personal learning project** built to practice advanced frontend architecture, high-frequency browser animations, canvas telemetry simulators, and client-side cryptographic state.

---

## 🛠️ Purpose & Educational Goals
The main goal of this repository is to build hands-on experience with:
- **Interactive State Engines**: Orchestrating complex local state machines to simulate multiple interconnected components (wearable devices, scanners, live filters, and audit logs).
- **Advanced UI Design & Micro-Animations**: Constructing a high-contrast Cyberpunk / Tactical Tech interface using Tailwind CSS and Framer Motion (`motion/react`) for a professional, tactile look and feel.
- **Simulated Signal & DSP Processing**: Practicing digital signal processing concepts via Web Audio API frequency scramblers, Kalman filtering mathematical simulations, and dynamic spectrum analyzers.
- **Client-Side Cryptography**: Exercising asymmetric cryptographic generation (Web Crypto API) for mock secure handshakes, rotating tokens, and signature verification.

## 📂 Core Features & Functional Architecture

BlurBubble provides a full-spectrum visual and acoustic protection simulation, structured as follows:

### 1. Unified Client & Wearable Beacon Management
- **Universal Beacon Linking**: Simulates the pairing of diverse, existing hardware devices (and potential specialized wearables) to broadcast the BlurBubble opt-out signal.
- **Rolling Ephemeral IDs**: Refreshes beacon signatures dynamically to prevent passive tracking, aligning with modern decentralized "Find My" protocols.

### 2. Live Intelligent Camera Overlay (Glasses HUD)
- **Face & Body Target Redaction**: A real-time camera viewfinder demonstrating live pixelation, blur filters, emoji covers, and holographic bounding cages around detected faces.
- **Interactive Coordinate Calibration**: Drag-and-drop focus offsets with Kalman noise filtering variables ($Q$ and $R$ parameters) simulating advanced targeting math.

### 3. Geofencing Zones (Physical Privacy Safeguards)
- **Entrance Automation (e.g., Primary Schools)**: Anchors broadcast specific geofenced rules. When children in school uniform enter a pre-registered "School Zone", compliant recording devices (smart-glasses, smartphones) automatically apply blurred masks at the hardware ISP level.
- **Private Events & Corporate Spaces**: Organizations can define geofences protecting proprietary display screens, art installations, or sensitive documents. Non-compliant lenses automatically capture redacted screens, protecting trade secrets without intrusive barriers.

### 4. Acoustic Scrambler & Media Indexing Crawler
- **Psychoacoustic Sound Scrambler**: Employs real-time Web Audio oscillators to emit high-frequency auditory guards or spectral inversions, demonstrating physical audio recording jammer logic.
- **Media Compliance Audit Spider**: Simulates a high-efficiency cloud crawler that scans directories across major streaming indexes (Spotify, SoundCloud, Apple Podcasts) and social video networks (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Facebook). It detects raw face hashes or vocal watermarks and triggers automated retroactive blur requests or takedown compliance orders.

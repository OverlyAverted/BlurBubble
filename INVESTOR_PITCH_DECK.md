## Slide 2: The Solution — The Universal Compliance Shield

### BlurBubble (Stop Recording Me)
BlurBubble is a proactive, secure, and decentralized privacy shield that puts physical control back into the hands of citizens:
1.  **Wearable Signal Broadcasting**: A sleek hardware clip-on transmits dynamic, short-range compliance directives to nearby digital lenses using unlicensed 2.4GHz bands. Adapts dynamically to iOS (iBeacon background packets) and Android (Eddystone-EID secure Knox offloader).
2.  **Web Bluetooth Synchronization**: Integrates native browser-side `navigator.bluetooth` GATT characteristic communication, allowing direct clock calibration and cryptographic key exchanges without requiring app-store downloads.
3.  **W3C DID Consent Whitelisting**: Employs Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs) and cryptographic Verifiable Credentials (VCs). This allows authorized family vloggers, friends, or school systems to bypass facial blurring dynamically without exposing sensitive identity databases to centralized servers.
4.  **Edge-Level Redaction**: Complying cameras automatically identify the signal, isolate the target coordinates, and apply localized real-time censorship overlays (Gaussian blurring, emoji replacement, or background inpainting) before saving or processing frames.
5.  **Zero-Knowledge Authentication**: Face-mesh vectors are encrypted and compared purely locally, meaning user identities are never transmitted, aggregated, or exposed in centralized corporate databases.
